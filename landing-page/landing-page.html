Understanding Alzheimer's Disease

Alzheimer's disease, characterized by memory loss and cognitive decline, lacks a cure. However, treatments like cognitive games and medications can slow its progression. Support from caregivers and CogniCare's holistic approach, offering games, medications, and community support, empowers individuals to manage symptoms and improve their quality of life.
